Snowboarding

Snowboarding is my favorite activity in the world - if I could do it for a living, I would in a heartbeat.

I have been snowboarding since I was 10, and ride very proficiently in trees and on steep bowls.

I tend to stay away from tricks due to injuries, but once I get in the trees, I always love to send it!

My favorite mountain I've ridden is Big Sky in Montana, and my favorite East Coast mountain is Mont Tremblant in Quebec.
